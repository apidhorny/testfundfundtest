About the Ivan Lozowy Charitable Fund:

The founder and director of the Ivan Hryhorovych Lozowy Charitable Fund is Ivan Lozowy, who was born in the USA, moved to Ukraine in 1991 and received Ukrainian citizenship in 1997. For the past few years the Fund has paid special attention to Ivan Hryhorovych 's â€œsmall motherland'' - Sumshchyna, supporting educational and cultural institutions, museums, artists and civic activists.

According to the Charter of the Fund, the purpose of its activities is to carry out charitable activities in the field of assistance to the Ukrainian military, in the implementation of public initiatives aimed at the formation of civil society and democracy in Ukraine, the development of territorial communities. The fund is named after Ivan Lozowy's father, who was born in the Sumshchyna, served in the British Army in Germany and moved to the United States, where he started a family and successfully set up his own real estate repair and investment business. The Ivan Hryhorovych Lozowy Charitable Fund was registered as a public organization on April 24, 2015. On December 17, 2015, the Fund was granted the status of a non-profit organization and entered in the State Register of Non-Profit Institutions and Organizations.
